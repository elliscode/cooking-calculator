Ingredient Converter

Use natural language to convert your ingredients to other units

How does it work?

Simply write in the amount of your ingredient, and what you would like to convert to, and the calculator will take care of the conversion!

You can even convert from volume units, such as cups, to mass units, such as grams, if you provide an ingredient name with a known density.

Why did you make this?

As an avid home baker, I am always looking up new recipes, and I have found that they are not always in the friendliest ingredient format. For instance, I have no way of measuring mL in my kitchen, and I am often opening up several searches on how to convert from one unit to another. In the hustle and bustle of baking, I don't always get my math correct, which has led to several ruined meals and desserts.

Now with the Ingredient Converter, I can simply type in what the recipe states for a given ingredient, and what I would like to measure with, whether that be a mass unit such as grams, or a volume unit such as cups. As long as the ingredient you are using is in our ingredient database with a density, the tool will be able to convert between mass and volume units without issue.

Why does my result say ???

This means you were trying to convert between mass and volume units for a food with an unknown density. This tool has a list of ingredient densities that it uses to convert between mass and volume units, and if a density is not known, it defaults to an unknown result of ???.
