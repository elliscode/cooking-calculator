Ingredient Converter

Use natural language to convert your ingredients to other units

How does it work?

Simply write in the amount of your ingredient, and what you would like to convert to, and the calculator will take care of the conversion!

You can even convert from volume units, such as cups, to mass units, such as grams, if you provide an ingredient name with a known density.

Why does my result say ???

This means you were trying to convert between mass and volume units for a food with an unknown density. This tool has a list of ingredient densities that it uses to convert between mass and volume units, and if a density is not known, it defaults to an unknown result of ???.
